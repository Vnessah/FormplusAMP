Auto-Renewal

Formplus is a recurring subscription service and will continue to renew until you cancel. It auto-renews at the end of a subscription period (30 days for monthly plan, 365 days for yearly plan). To prevent auto-renewal, cancel your subscription before the end of your current subscription period.

Cancel Anytime

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. All these options are available from your Account page or can be done by contacting support

30 Day Money Back Guarantee

If for any reason you decide you want to cancel your account within 30 days of your payment, we will issue a full refund if you have not received any responses.

Customer Support

Apart from our support center, we provide 24/7 email support services to all customers. You can submit a ticket or email support@formpl.us to contact our support team. Support emails are typically responded to within 2 hours.

Nonprofit Discount

As a way of saying thank you to nonprofit organizations, eligible users can apply for a 20% discount off any of our plans including the yearly plans which already comes with a 20% discount. If you represent a nonprofit organization, contact us to request for the nonprofit discount.

Education Discount

Formplus is proud to support our educators and school administrators. Education users qualify for a 20% discount off any of our plans including the yearly plans which already comes with a 20% discount. If you are an educator, contact us to request for the educational discount.
